Kwiqpick doesn't make its customers wait. It simply allows diners to check-in at any table of a participating dining outlet - restaurant, pub, lounge, movie theatre, hotel - and start ordering ones food and drinks. It also provides useful data to the dining outlet to make it more efficient.

Where we started?

Every time we visited a restaurant, we were hungry and waiting. And when we decided todo something about it, we also got to know that reducing this waiting time reduces food wastage as well. Our company was founded in 2016 for the same intention.

Kwiqpick will now provide an easy to setup, simple solution, which will reduce customer's waiting time. This simple solution promises to deliver a quicker revenue increase and encourage loyalty to every dining outlet.

How do we do it?

Automation - Automation is the future of every kitchen. Kwiqpick provides an Android Mobile Application through which customers can simply check-in, view a LIVE MENU and start ordering! Once done, customers can pay simply pay online using the app.

Kwiqpick also provides a web interface for managers to manage their dining outlet even better. Kwiqpick will provide you with real-time useful data to optimize your operations, reduce overhead costs, increase table turnover and higher revenues. It also takes care of the incoming order queue, so that your Chef can now do what they really like to do.
